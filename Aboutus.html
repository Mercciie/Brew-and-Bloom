Get to know us and our story

Here at Brew & Bloom Café, we have a firm belief that every cup tells a story. Nesting in the heart of Kempton Park, our little corner café is seen as a safe haven for your coffee lovers,
flower enthusiasts and anyone who is just looking for a moment of calmness in the middle of their noisy days.
In here, the rich aroma of freshly brewed coffee mingles with the gentle fragrance of seasonal blooms, creating a space where every visit feels like a breath of fresh air.


Our Story:


Brew & Bloom began as just a vision and a dream, which was to bring people together with two of life’s great pleasures — beautiful flowers and a great cup of coffee.
Founded by two mates who shared a love for artisanal brewing and botanic design, we’re happy to say that our café from love and a passion into a thriving community hub.


Our promise:


We have committed ourselves to serving ethically sourced, locally roasted coffee and freshly baked goods that are made with love.
With flowers handpicked from our local growers, we look to support small businesses whilst ensuring the freshest, most vibrant arrangements for your table.
Sustainability, community and creativity guides everything that we do.


We are more than just a café:


Whether you’re here for your morning latte, catching up with a friend, an afternoon escape or one of our lively Friday night music sessions,
Brew & Bloom’s got you because we’re more than just a place to grab a drink, we’re an experience. Brew & Bloom is a place to connect, recharge and feel inspired.


Come chill and bloom with us:


Step inside and find yourself a cozy corner so you can allow the world around you to slow down for a little while.
Our team can’t wait to welcome you to our lively café, where every sip blooms into a smile.
